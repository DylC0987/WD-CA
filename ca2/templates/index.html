How To play

  • Use the arrow keys to move your soldier and avoid the ghosts.
  • Move your player to start the game
  • Collect discoballs to activate "Bullet Time" and gain plasma bullets for 10 seconds.
  • When Bullet Time is active, use w, a, s, d keys to aim your gun. (w = up) (s = down) (a = left) (d = right) and use spacebar to shoot.
  • Kill as many ghosts as you can to raise your score(Kill Count)
  • Game ends when the player is touched by a ghost.
  • For test purposes press "p" to make the player invincible to ghost collisions."p" pressed again will toggle off this cheat

Ghosts Info

  • Ghosts can move through obstacles, but doing so slows them down.
  • When ghosts are white, they are at their slowest and least aggressive.
  • When you kill enough of their ghost friends they will turn orange and become more aggresive.
  • When they turn red, they are at their most aggressive state .
